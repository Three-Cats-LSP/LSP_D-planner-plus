Average Depth — Planning Converter
Can you use average depth to plan a dive? Yes — with caveats. RDP tables assume a square profile (max depth the whole dive), so average depth gives you more credit than you actually used — safer for planning a single dive. However for repetitive dives, fast compartments are driven by your actual maximum depth, not the average — so using average depth to calculate surface intervals can be unconservative. This tool converts your log-book average depth to a recommended planning depth that balances both effects.
